Overview

Voidexpunged is a mysterious and powerful entity shrouded in darkness and myth. Revered and feared across countless realms, he is said to be the living embodiment of the void itself, erasing all that stands in his path. His presence is marked by an unsettling silence and a chilling aura that warps reality.

Phases

Default

The primary form of Voidexpunged. His face is a striking, glowing red mark set against a void-black silhouette, giving him an unsettling and enigmatic presence. The mark is not a symbol, but his actual face, radiating with an eerie intensity from the darkness.

Apop (Angered Form)

When Voidexpunged is angered, he morphs into this more menacing and volatile form, radiating intense wrath.

Decaying

A rare state where Voidexpunged appears to be deteriorating, his form breaking apart and losing cohesion.

Phase 2 (Enraged)

In this phase, Voidexpunged becomes truly enraged, unleashing the full extent of his void powers and becoming even more formidable.

Alpha (Developer Only)

The alpha version of voidexpunged. This is the first version of voidexpunged ever created.

Abilities

  • Void Erasure: Can erase objects, beings, and even memories from existence.
  • Reality Distortion: Warps space and time around him, making him nearly impossible to predict or contain.
  • Intangible Form: Physical attacks pass through him as if he were a shadow.
  • Fear Inducement: His presence instills overwhelming dread and confusion.
  • Cosmic Awareness: Senses disturbances and anomalies across dimensions.

Personality

Voidexpunged is silent, enigmatic, and seemingly emotionless. He rarely communicates, preferring to let his actions speak. Despite his terrifying powers, he is not overtly malicious; his motives are inscrutable, and some believe he acts to maintain a cosmic equilibrium, erasing only those who threaten the balance of the Voidverse.

Trivia

  • His face is rumored to be a warning to all who see it.
  • No one has ever heard him speak.
  • He leaves behind a faint, static-like glitchy leftover after expunging something.
  • Some theorize he is immune to all forms of magic and technology.
  • He is often depicted in ancient texts as a shadow with a crimson face.
